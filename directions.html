Directions to WhoDunnit Shows

Bristol Bar & Grille
614 W. Main Street
Louisville, KY 40202
Phone: 502-582-1995

From the North via I-65

I-65 South to Louisville, Exit 137 for I-64 West and then take Exit 5B for 3rd Street. At the end of the off ramp, turn right onto River Road and then take second left onto 6th Street. Take first right onto Main Street and Bristol Bar & Grille is on your left. OR, continue straight on 6th Street and park in the PARC garage on the right about half a block after crossing Main Street. Parking is free (with validation) from Bristol Bar & Grille on Saturdays after 6:00 p.m.

From the South via I-65

I-65 North to Brook Street Exit 136B and merge onto Brook Street and then turn left onto E. Main Street until you get to 6th Street. Bristol Bar & Grille is on your left after crossing 6th Street. OR, turn left onto 6th Street and park in the PARC garage on the right about half a block after turning from Main Street. Parking is free (with validation) from Bristol Bar & Grille on Saturdays after 6:00 p.m.

From the West via I-64

Follow I-64 East towards Louisville, then take the 9th Street/ Roy Wilkins exit, Exit 4, toward downtown. Keep left to take Market St./9th Street ramp toward Jefferson Street. Turn left onto W Market Street. Turn left onto S 5th Street and then take the first left onto West Main Street. Bristol Bar & Grille is on your left after crossing 6th Street. OR, turn left onto 6th Street and park in the PARC garage on the right about half a block after turning from Main Street. Parking is free (with validation) from Bristol Bar & Grille on Saturdays after 6:00 p.m.

WhoDunnit is a non-profit, donation-supported corporation for the performing arts.
WhoDunnit doesn't license scripts directly. However, most of our critically acclaimed and very popular murder mystery and dinner theater shows are available for licensing directly from the authors. More than 30 titles are available. For a list of available scripts and licensing info, phone us at (502) 426-7100.

Please note, however, that WhoDunnit authors consider mystery to be an art form. If your group does over-the-top comedic mysteries with minimal story line, please do not call us. However, if you would like to thrill your audiences with scripts worthy of A&E, we welcome your inquiry: (502) 426-7100
