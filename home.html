WhoDunnit shows are original, lively and thoroughly enertaining, as well as a mystery lover's dream!

Clues are cleverly included in the scripted action, and in between scenes, as the audience enjoys a fine meal, the actors mingle with the audience in character, allowing guests to ask questions and get more clues. If you ask them if they are the killer, they will all say no, but that is the only time they are permitted to lie. To all other questions, they must either evade the question or tell you the truth.

Audiences love interacting with the characters and trying to figure out who dunnit!

WhoDunnit is a non-profit, donation-supported corporation for the performing arts.
WhoDunnit doesn't license scripts directly. However, most of our critically acclaimed and very popular murder mystery and dinner theater shows are available for licensing directly from the authors. More than 30 titles are available. For a list of available scripts and licensing info, phone us at (502) 426-7100.

Please note, however, that WhoDunnit authors consider mystery to be an art form. If your group does over-the-top comedic mysteries with minimal story line, please do not call us. However, if you would like to thrill your audiences with scripts worthy of A&E, we welcome your inquiry: (502) 426-7100
